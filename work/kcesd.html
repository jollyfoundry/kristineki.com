KCESD

Brand Identity / Digital

A modern identity system designed to unify communication and strengthen organizational clarity.

Overview

A refreshed brand identity and digital system created to support KCESD’s mission, improve communication, and deliver a cohesive visual presence across all touchpoints.

Challenge

Unify a fragmented visual identity and create a system that feels modern, accessible, and aligned with the organization’s values.

Approach

Lead with clarity and structure. Build a flexible identity system supported by clean typography, a functional color palette, and a digital framework that scales.

Deliverables

Brand identity, logo system, website design, visual guidelines, digital templates.

Process

Developed a modular identity system rooted in accessibility and consistency—clear grids, structured layouts, and a visual language that supports communication at every level.

Design

The final identity balances professionalism and approachability—clean typography, modern iconography, and a digital system built for clarity and ease of use.

Outcome

A cohesive brand and digital system that strengthens KCESD’s communication, improves usability, and supports long‑term organizational growth.
