Abbott Privacy

Campaign / Corporate

A corporate campaign designed to communicate data privacy with clarity, trust, and authority.

Overview

A corporate communication system built to simplify complex privacy concepts and reinforce Abbott’s commitment to protecting user data.

Challenge

Translate technical privacy requirements into a clear, accessible, and visually coherent campaign for a global audience.

Approach

Lead with simplicity and trust. Build a visual system rooted in clarity, supported by clean typography, structured layouts, and approachable iconography.

Deliverables

Campaign identity, visual system, iconography, digital assets, internal communication materials.

Process

Developed a modular communication system that balances corporate authority with human clarity—clean grids, intuitive visuals, and messaging that builds confidence.

Design

The final system uses structured layouts, bold clarity, and a calm visual tone to communicate complex information in a way that feels accessible and trustworthy.

Outcome

A clear, modern communication system that strengthens Abbott’s privacy messaging and supports global understanding of data protection practices.
