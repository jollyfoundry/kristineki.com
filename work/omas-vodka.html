Oma’s Vodka

Brand Identity / Packaging

A handcrafted identity and packaging system rooted in heritage, craft, and authenticity.

Overview

A premium spirits identity built to honor tradition while introducing a modern, elevated visual presence. The system blends heritage storytelling with clean, contemporary design.

Challenge

Create a packaging system that feels handcrafted and authentic while standing out in a crowded premium spirits market.

Approach

Lead with texture, warmth, and clarity. Build a visual system that balances artisanal cues with modern structure and premium detail.

Deliverables

Brand identity, packaging design, label system, visual language, production files.

Process

Explored handcrafted typography, textured materials, and a restrained color palette to create a system that feels rooted in heritage yet unmistakably modern.

Design

The final identity blends clean structure with expressive detail—premium typography, balanced composition, and subtle craft cues that elevate the brand.

Outcome

A premium packaging system that communicates authenticity, elevates shelf presence, and positions Oma’s Vodka as a modern craft spirit with deep roots.
