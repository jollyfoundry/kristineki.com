McCormick Seasonal

Campaign / Seasonal

A seasonal campaign crafted to deliver color, flavor, and cultural relevance.

Overview

A vibrant seasonal campaign built to celebrate flavor, spark curiosity, and bring McCormick’s products to life during key cultural moments.

Challenge

Create a seasonal identity that feels fresh and expressive while staying true to McCormick’s trusted brand heritage.

Approach

Lead with bold color, playful illustration, and expressive typography to capture the energy of the season and the spirit of the brand.

Deliverables

Seasonal campaign, visual identity, packaging extensions, digital assets.

Process

Explored expressive seasonal motifs, dynamic compositions, and a flexible visual system that adapts across packaging, digital, and in‑store displays.

Design

The final system blends bold seasonal color with clean structure, creating a campaign that feels both festive and unmistakably McCormick.

Outcome

A cohesive seasonal campaign that enhances brand visibility, drives engagement, and brings McCormick’s products to life during key cultural moments.
