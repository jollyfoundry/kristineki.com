Granny’s

Brand Identity / Packaging

A warm, expressive identity system designed to modernize a nostalgic brand without losing its charm.

Overview

A refreshed identity and packaging system created to bring new life to a beloved heritage brand. The system blends nostalgia with modern clarity to appeal to both longtime fans and new audiences.

Challenge

Update a legacy brand in a way that feels fresh and relevant while preserving the emotional connection that defines its history.

Approach

Lead with warmth and simplicity. Build a visual system rooted in expressive typography, approachable color, and clean structure.

Deliverables

Brand identity, packaging system, visual language, production files, digital assets.

Process

Explored nostalgic visual cues, handcrafted typography, and a flexible layout system that balances heritage with modern clarity.

Design

The final identity blends expressive character with clean structure—warm color, friendly typography, and a packaging system built for clarity and shelf impact.

Outcome

A modernized brand system that strengthens recognition, enhances shelf presence, and preserves the emotional warmth that defines Granny’s.
