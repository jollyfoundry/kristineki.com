Calypso

Packaging / Brand Identity

A vibrant packaging system designed to amplify flavor, energy, and shelf‑level impact.

Overview

A bold packaging refresh created to elevate Calypso’s iconic flavors with a modern, high‑energy visual system. The design brings clarity, vibrancy, and consistency across the full product line.

Challenge

Strengthen brand recognition and unify a diverse flavor portfolio while maintaining the expressive personality fans love.

Approach

Lead with color and structure. Build a scalable system that balances expressive flavor cues with clean, modern hierarchy.

Deliverables

Packaging system, visual identity, flavor extensions, production files, digital assets.

Process

Explored bold color palettes, expressive illustration, and a modular layout system that adapts seamlessly across dozens of SKUs.

Design

The final system delivers clarity and vibrancy—clean structure paired with energetic flavor cues and a modernized brand voice.

Outcome

A cohesive packaging system that enhances shelf presence, strengthens brand recognition, and supports ongoing flavor expansion.
